Vegan Morning Glory Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 3/4 cup applesauce
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups oat flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3/4 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 3/4 cups grated carrot
  • 1 apple grated
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • handful chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • pumpkin seeds for topping

Directions

  1. Prepare flax egg by mixing water and flaxseed. Let sit for 5 minutes
  2. Set oven to 350 F and grease muffin tin with olive oil
  3. n bowl of stand mixer combine maple syrup, applesauce, olive oil, and chia egg until well combined
  4. In separate bowl mix oat flour, salt, spices, baking powder and baking soda
  5. Place sieve over the bowl with wet ingredients and sift dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Mix well
  6. Fold in raisins, grated carrot, apple and nuts
  7. Divide batter between the 12 muffin holes and sprinkle with nuts and seeds
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let them cool before removing from tin.
